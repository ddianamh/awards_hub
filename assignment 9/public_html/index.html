Just one click away from learning more about Grammy winners and awarded music!
Awards Hub is a one-stop resource intended for learning about the tradition of the Grammy Awards and celebrating the artists who have performed on stages throughout the world. If you are curious about some of the latest and most loved melodies, albums, or visual creations, then check out our tabs for learning more about winners and nominees! Here is how to use our page: On the Imprint Page, you can find contact details and the GDPR disclaimer. If you click on any of the Awards, Music, Artists pages, you will be able to find out more about the winners of the latest Grammy's. Now, if you want to add musical recommendations that you consider worthy of being nominated at the Grammy's, you can do so from the User Input page. You can add a song, an album, a singer, a band, you can even add new ideas for awards!
